Why Horse Power

Why choose Horse Power? Our drink is made from roasted gram flour, packed with essential nutrients, and formulated for sustained energy. Whether you’re a gym-goer, techie, or simply health-conscious, Horse Power gives you the natural boost you need to power through your day. It's not just a drink—it's a commitment to better health, day by day.

Natural Coolant

Horse Power is renowned for its cooling properties, which can help regulate body temperature. In the weather of Bengaluru consuming Horse Power can provide a refreshing effect, reducing the risk of heat strokes and dehydration.

Nutrient-Rich

Horse Power is a powerhouse of essential nutrients, It contains a good amount of Protein, which is vital for muscle repair and growth. The fibre content aids in digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness.

Energy boost

The combination of protein and fibre in Horse Power provides sustained energy release throughout the day. Unlike high-sugar snacks, Horse Power offers a steady supply of energy, helping you stay active and alert.

Digestive Health

Horse Power is high in dietary fibre, which is essential for healthy digestion. Fibre helps regulate bowel movements, preventing constipation and promotes a healthy gut.

Weight Management

Horse Power has a low glycemic index, meaning it releases sugar slowly into the bloodstream. This helps in controlling blood sugar levels and curbing hunger pangs, making it a great option for weight management.

Detoxification

Horse Power acts as a natural detoxifying agent. It helps cleanse the intestines and remove accumulated toxins from the body.

Heart Health

Horse Power is beneficial for cardiovascular health due to its magnesium content. Magnesium helps regulate blood pressure and maintain a healthy heart rhythm.

Diabetes Friendly

Horse Power's low glycemic index means it doesn't cause rapid spikes in blood sugar levels. It provide a steady release of glucose, making it a suitable and safe option for people with diabetes.
